## Slide 2 · עולם ה-AI — 0:05–0:10 (5 min)

**Say:**
- AI isn't one thing — it's a whole family of machines, and each type is built differently and works differently.
- Classic software: the computer follows an exact recipe a human wrote. Calculator, traffic light, Excel. Zero learning.
- Machine learning: nobody wrote the recipe — it learned patterns from millions of examples. Your phone unlocking on your face. The spam filter. **Waze predicting traffic** (they know Waze — use it).
- Recommendation engines: learned what you liked, guesses what you want next. Netflix, YouTube — that's why you can't stop scrolling.
- Generative AI: the new kid. Doesn't just recognize — CREATES new things: text, images, voice, video.

**Must land:** "each type works differently" — so ChatGPT's quirks (next slide) make sense.

**Transition:** "Tonight's star is the type that creates TEXT. It's called an LLM — and here's the trick behind it."

## Slide 3 · אז מה זה LLM? — 0:10–0:16 (6 min)

**Say:**
- LLM = Large Language Model. A model trained on unbelievable amounts of text to do ONE thing: guess the next word. "בירת צרפת היא ___" — everyone in this room finished that sentence. That's the whole trick.
- Do that trick at enormous scale and something surprising happens: it can write, summarize, translate, plan, hold a conversation. No magic, no consciousness — very, very good prediction that FEELS like understanding.
- The players: OpenAI makes ChatGPT. Anthropic makes Claude (that's what I work with all day). Google — Gemini. Meta — Llama. ChatGPT is a product, the technology is bigger than one company. Like Waze vs Google Maps — same idea, different flavors.
- **Plant this now (pays off later):** because it's a prediction machine, it ALWAYS produces something that sounds right. Sounding right and being right are not the same thing. Hold that thought — I'll prove it to you soon.

**Must land:** sounds right ≠ is right. Say it slowly.

**Transition:** "OK. So that's the machine. Now — be honest — how do you actually use it?"
